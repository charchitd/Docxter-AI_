## ❌ The Problem: The High Cost of Generic AI & Data Leaks

Generic cloud-based LLM assistants (ChatGPT Plus, Notion AI) present two critical challenges for modern business operations:

1. 🔒 **Confidentiality Leaks:** Uploading private corporate ledgers, tax records, and client supplier lists to external cloud servers risks serious compliance and data leaks.
2. 💰 **High Fixed Costs:** Subscription structures demand high, rigid monthly costs regardless of whether the AI generates concrete savings or profit for the business.

---

## ⚡ The Docxter Value: Privacy-First & Performance-Linked

Docxter AI is custom-engineered to solve these challenges for Indian wholesalers, distributors, and UK SMBs:

* 🖥️ **100% Offline Local Chat:** Processes ledgers locally on your office computer using **Google Gemma 4** (via Ollama) and pgvector. Your files never leave your system.
* 💰 **₹0 / £0 Cloud Bills:** Zero operational API overhead in offline mode.
* 📈 **Performance-Based Pricing:** We only bill you a 3.5% commission on verified savings our AI finds (GST/VAT recovery, overbilled supplier rate benchmark gaps, deadwood stock).

## 🚀 Key Premium Features

### 1. 📊 DCLI (Core Ledger Intelligence)
Our autonomous vCFO synthesizer engine scans historic business transactions to detect cost gaps:
* **Vendor Rate Benchmarks:** Flags when current quotes exceed past purchase benchmarks.
* **GST/VAT Reconciliation:** Cross-references invoices with tax statements to claim 100% of unclaimed credits.
* **Deadwood Inventory Detector:** Identifies slow-moving capital locked in warehouses.

### 2. ⚡ Hybrid Online/Offline State Toggle
Effortlessly switch between lightning-fast cloud Gemini models (utilizing free tier rules) and 100% private local Gemma 4 with a single toggle in the workspace.

### 3. 🗣️ Commercial-Grade Chat Console
* Citations grounding every single response directly back to uploaded source document chunks.
* Dynamic SVG graph generator drawing business metrics in real-time.
* 1-click PO/invoice Excel exports.
